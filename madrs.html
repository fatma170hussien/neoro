Rating Instructions

The rating is based on a clinical interview. The rater must decide whether the rating lies on the defined scale steps (0, 2, 4, 6) or between them (1, 3, 5).

Rating Guidelines

  • Rate based on clinical interview and observation
  • Consider intensity, frequency, duration, and functional impact
  • Use even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6) for defined scale steps
  • Use odd numbers (1, 3, 5) for intermediate ratings
  • The scale can be used for any time interval between ratings
