Time Frame

This assessment evaluates anxiety symptoms experienced over the past two weeks, providing a snapshot of recent anxiety levels.

Clinical Cut-off

A score of 10 or higher suggests clinically significant anxiety symptoms that may warrant further evaluation.

Reliability & Validity

The GAD-7 has excellent psychometric properties with high sensitivity and specificity for detecting GAD in primary care settings.
