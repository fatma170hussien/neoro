Clinical Significance

A score of 10 or higher suggests clinically significant depression that may warrant professional evaluation.

Scores 15+ indicate more severe depression requiring clinical attention.

Diagnostic Tool

The PHQ-9 maps directly to the 9 DSM-5 criteria for major depressive disorder, making it both a severity measure and diagnostic aid.
